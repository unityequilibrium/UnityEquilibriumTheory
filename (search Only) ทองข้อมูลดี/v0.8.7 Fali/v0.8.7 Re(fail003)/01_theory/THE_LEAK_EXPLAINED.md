When we say standard theories have a "Leak", we mean they **ignore** a critical part of reality to make the math easier. UET captures that missing part.

## 1. The Finance Leak: "Liquidity"
*   **The Leak:** Standard models (Black-Scholes) assume you can *always* sell your stock for the market price.
*   **Reality:** In a crash, **Liquidity Leaks Out**. There are NO buyers. The "pipe" runs dry.
*   **The Flaw:** Models calculate risk assuming a full pipe. When the pipe empties, the model crashes (e.g., 2008 Crisis).
*   **UET Fix:** We model the "Fluid" itself. If $k$ drops, we know the fluid is evaporating. We predict the dry pipe.

## 2. The Physics Leak: "Infinity"
*   **The Leak:** When you try to combine Gravity and Quantum Mechanics, the math explodes to **Infinity**.
*   **The Fix (Old):** Physicists use "Renormalization" (a math trick) to subtract the infinity. They basically "mop up the leak" and pretend it wasn't there.
*   **UET Fix:** We don't subtract. We show that Gravity ($k=1$) and Quantum ($k=2$) are just different **Flow States** of the same fluid. No leak to mop up.

## 3. The Neuroscience Leak: "Noise"
*   **The Leak:** Brain scientists usually filter out "Background Noise" to see the neuron spike. They treat $90\%$ of brain activity as "garbage" (leaking it out of their data).
*   **Reality:** That "Noise" is the **Signal**. It follows a $1/f^2$ pattern (Brownian Motion).
*   **UET Fix:** We analyze the noise (Spectrum). We found the "Thinking" is actually in the "Leaked Noise" they threw away.
